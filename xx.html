Behind the Lens

I'm Aditya Kushwaha, a visual storyteller driven by passion and light. I believe that every photograph is a conversation between light, shadow, and the soul of the subject — a fleeting truth frozen in time.

My work spans portraits, landscapes, weddings, and street photography. Each frame is crafted with intention, honoring the moment as it truly was.
